About

Crazy about fans? Us too! Fanning Over Fans is the #4 fansite dedicated to all things fans! An often overlooked piece of household equipment, the fan has seen a remarkable evolution in form, from the fan palms of the ancient world to to the contemporary pieces we find today.

Here at Fanning Over Fans, we present to you some of our favorite fans with the hope that you will enjoy some of their unique qualities as much as we do. Check them out below!

Table Fans

Vornado V-Fan

Originally released in 1945, this little piece of rotary equipment challenged how a fan should look and function. It's sleek shape is aerodynamically designed for packing power into its petite frame. A perfection edition for those with retro inspirations.

Urbanjet Fan

Another retro-inspired desk fan, the Urbanjet Fan is inspire by the 1957 Chevrolet. When the breeze from this baby runs through your hair, you'll feel like you're cruising down Route 66 straight to the West Coast!

Caframo Chinook

Cage free and easy to clean. Who can't appreciate a fan that doesn't require dusting? Also, life is a bit more fun when you live on the edge of the danger...

Ceiling Fans

Jet Engine Ceiling Fan

Some people want to emit the feeling of a gentle ocean breeze with their ceiling arrangements. Others want to feel like they are on the tarmac right behind a fleet of 757s. This fan is for that latter group.

Ribbon Ceiling Fan

Although this bad boy was really only a conceptual piece, it's still a favorite for its flowing form. Hopefully we'll see this in real home one day.

Lumio Bladesless Ceiling Fan

Bladeless fans are all the rage these days. With its elegant design, this modern day fixture brings the perfect balance to style and performance.

Tower Fans

Ozari 3x Tower Fan

Defining the latest in stylistic innovation and digital decor, this Ozari masterpiece is among the thinnest fans in the world. Quaint and quiet, you won't hear this gem running through the night.

Dyson AM07 Tower Fan

The OG of bladeless fans, Dyson consistently boasts the latest in fan technology. A true classic, this modern marvel shows that the future of fans remain bright.

LivePure Blade Tower Fan

Why have blades when you can be the blade? This tall thin tower provides a smooth stream of airflow to bring comfort, while looking suave in whatever corner you put it in.

Hand Fans

Traditional Folding Hand Fan

A true test of time, the foldable hand fan can be traced back to both Ancient Greece and China. A multi-functional piece of equipment, the foldable fan has been known to be used in the performances of traditional Kung Fu, Flamenco, and nowadays at your local club or rave.

Misting Hand Fan

Real fans of fans will know what these little gadgets are all about it. If you've never felt the cooling sensation of a misting fan that fits in your pocket on a hot summer's day, well, you haven't lived my friend.
